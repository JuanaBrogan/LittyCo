About us

Just a fitness obsessed couple who are about empowering others to be their OWN unique selves. Creating a distinct brand that blends our own fun personality and stands out in the masses of common lifestyle bands. Be yourself everywhere you go and celebrate along the way. We want to inspire others to live in the moment and be proud of who they are. Separate yourself from the crowd by being courageous in situations when it calls for a hero, even if a hero is just lending a helping hand!

Coach Cheyenne

You can call me Coach Chey. I am a former collegiete and professional basketball player. Starting right here in Southern California then making my way to Chicago, Boston then traveling all the way to Guangzhou, China. Despite my basketball travels, my training career first started in Boston, where I was surrounded by outstandingly bright and motivated individuals. This birthed a fire inside of me to keep learning and helping individuals train for their sports correctly so they can preform to their best ability. Though I train with athletes, my passion doesn't stop there. Helping the every day person to be functional and confident in their daily lives is something thats very important to me. I am certified in NASM, NCSA, NFPT, AED and currently coaching at a collegiate level for basketball. Weather your'e a professional athlete looking to improve performance, an actress/actor looking to improve their physique or even a stay at home mom/dad looking to feel strong, sexy and confident- lets connect! Through my diverse training background and passion to help those around me to thrive and improve their lifestyles, I'm ready to be on this journey with you!

Coach Alex

Former professional basketball player turned into a fitness coach. After a tragic achilles rupture I had to take my selfcare even more seriuosly. Luckily, I was surrounded by extremely smart and talented trainers during my recovery. After only 5 months I was able to run and move on the court, but I knew that I needed to take what I learned and share it with more people. My training style is based on the fundamental philospohy that 'everyone is an athlete'. We run around with kids, we carry bags, we walk up stairs, etc. So training should be built on this foundation. Certified with NFPT, NPTI, TPI Rotational Sports, NASM PES, Licenced EMT & Current College Coach. No matter if you are a gym vet getting back into it or it is your first time going to the gym and looking for guidance with accountability; I am here with you in this process. We are in this together
